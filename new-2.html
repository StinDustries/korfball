Rules of Korfball

The main setup of Korfball

Two teams of eight players, with four boys and four girls each, face off against each other. The court is split up into 2 zones. In each zone is a Korfball hoop.

Offense and Defense

Teams must defend their determined defensive zone, and attack their opponent's. When a team is on offense, they cannot step on their defensive zone, and on defense cannot step on their offensive zone. On defense, players can only guard opposite players of thei own gender.
