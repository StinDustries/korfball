Rules of Korfball

Find a local Korfball club

  1. First, ensure that you live in England, as the site only has England coverage.
  2. Visit this site and find your town.
  3. Click on the closest green pin to your town.
  4. Visit the website linked in the pop-up
  5. Follow any rules they have to sign up, and go from there.
