Korfball

A sport that needs more recognition

Korfball is a Dutch sport created in 1902 by one Nico Broekhuysen. It is much like netball or basketball, in which the main goal is to throw a ball into a 3.5 meter tall hoop.

A Korf, the hoop they use in korfball. From https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Dmk8S-ZXsAAPJoZ.jpg

Modern Korfball

Today, Korfball is popular in Taiwan, Netherlands and Belgium,having 100,000 people playing in the Netherlands alone. Korfball clubs are popular in England and the Netherlands.
